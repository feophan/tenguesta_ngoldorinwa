<Aside type="quote" title="PE22/65 (2)">The 'dentals' *t, d, n, l, r* are 'point' consonants, but made as in English just behind the teeth.</Aside>

The "as in English" comment makes it clear the Quenya dentals were **alveolar**, and not, for example, denti-alveolar.
